Mac and Cheese

Best Mac and Cheese Recipe

This recipe has been handed down for generations in my family. Back from when cheese was first invented my family has been mastering the art of making mac and cheese. Through centuries of trial and error our family has finally crafted the perfect recipe for mac and cheese.

The process involves using the finest ingredients one can find at their locally sourced supermarket. You want a good blend of at least two types of cheddar cheeses and at least one semi soft cheese like gouda or munster. You will also need to use full butter and half & half milk. The richer the ingredients the more flavorful the mac and cheese will be.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups yellow cheddar
  • 2 cups white cheddar
  • 2 cups soft cheese (gouda or munster)
  • 32 ounces half & half
  • 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter
  • 1 tsp of Better than Bullion chicken
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 pound elbow macaroni

Directions

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Bring four quarts of salted water to a boil. Add in macaroni elbows and cook for 7 min until al dente. Remove and drain and set aside.
  3. In a separate pot melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, gradually add in the flour while whisking. You want the consistency to look like wet sand.
  4. In that same pot you want to slowly stir in the half and half. Then add in the garlic powder and bullion and stir until incorporated. Keep stirring until the consistency of thickened soup. Once achieved turn off stove.
  5. With the stove off add in once cup each of the yellow and white cheddar, and two cups of soft cheese. Using the residual heat melt the cheeses together until a uniform mixture is created.
  6. Take your cooked macaroni and add it to the pot with the cheese sauce and stir till evenly distributed. Once completed, transfer half of mixture into a 9x13 baking dish then cover with a mixture of yellow and white cheddar cheese. Lay down the remaining mixture and cover with remaining cheeses.
  7. Cover dish with aluminum foil and place in oven for 25 min or until edges begin to bubble.
  8. Remove from oven and serve warm.

I hope you enjoy making, as well as eating, this dish. Its super rich and flavorful and could possibly be on heavy rotation in your household.
